The Reader’s Charter

Rules of the Sanctuary

I.

Silence is a Sanctuary. Speak only in whispers, or let the voices on the pages be the only ones heard.

II.

Respect the Spine. Every volume is a vessel of history; handle each with the reverence it deserves.

III.

Analog Presence. Digital signals remain beyond the threshold. This is a space of focused presence.

IV.

Deep Focus. Reading is not a race but a journey. Linger on a single page as long as curiosity demands.

V.

Shared Wisdom. Marginalia is forbidden on our physical texts, but intellectual exchange is our lifeblood.

VI.

Legacy. You are a temporary guardian of these stories. Leave them better than you found them.

"In the presence of books, we are never truly alone."
